About H&S Associates

We are a premier sports public relations agency dedicated to shaping the stories that define the world of sports.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a sports journalist Himanshu Pareek and an expert Public Relations professional Sriparna, H&S Associates has grown from a boutique agency to one of the most respected names in sports PR. Our unique blend of media expertise and strategic communication has helped hundreds of clients navigate the complex sports media landscape.

We believe every athlete and sports organization has a compelling story to tell. Our job is to help you tell it in a way that resonates with fans, sponsors, and the media.

Our Services

Our Services

Comprehensive PR solutions tailored to the unique needs of the sports industry.

Media Relations

Strategic placement of stories in top sports media outlets, press conference coordination, and interview management to ensure your message reaches the right audience.

Brand Development

Crafting and maintaining powerful personal and organizational brands for athletes, teams, and sports organizations through consistent messaging and strategic positioning.

Crisis Management

Rapid response and strategic communication plans to protect reputations during challenging situations, with 24/7 availability for crisis support.

Event Publicity

Maximizing exposure for tournaments, product launches, and charity events through targeted media outreach and creative promotional strategies.

Media Training

Comprehensive training programs for athletes and executives to confidently handle media interviews, press conferences, and social media interactions.

Sponsorship Strategy

Developing compelling narratives and presentation materials to attract and retain sponsorship partners that align with your brand values.
